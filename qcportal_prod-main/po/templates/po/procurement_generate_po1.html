All necessary arrangement on this account will be made by you. In case replacement is delayed beyond three months of intimation cost of the material may be recovered from your pending bills/security deposit.

8.2.            The said material if required to be replaced, shall be collected by you from Area Stores/ work site at their own cost and at your own responsibility. These materials will like-wise be returned duly repaired/ replaced and tested subsequently by you to the destination indicated on at their cost in a reasonable time of days from the date of intimation. The guarantee period as stipulated in clause 8.1 above shall also be applicable for repaired/replaced material, which shall however be counted afresh from the date of its delivery in our stores/site.

8.3.            Further, it is clarified that all the charges towards carrying out repairs including packing, forwarding, loading, unloading shall be borne by you. The amount deposited under security deposit clause shall also cover the performance guarantee of the material.

8.4.            All equipment/ line materials reported failed within the specified guarantee period may be replaced free of cost by you.

8.5.            In case Equipment/material is installed at field and failed during the Guarantee period due to manufacturing defect, as per Clause 8.1, the actual cost of dismantling and replacement of these equipments/ materials with the new ones may be charged to you.

8.6.            To and fro transportation cost of such failed equipment may also be borne by you.

11. FAKE INSPECTION CALL

All such cases where after giving inspection call you will not offer full quantity as per your offer letter to the Inspecting Officer deputed by the Purchaser for any reason, shall be treated as ‘Fake Inspection Call’. The Purchaser will not be under any obligation to arrange inspection of such partial lot and you will be required to remit a sum of Rs. +GST as applicable for each fake inspection call.

In case of more than two Fake Inspection call under the same purchase order, the purchaser at its discretion may cancel the order for balance ordered quantity exercising provision of “Cancellation/ Termination of order” of the tender and also debar your firm from future business.

 

12. ACCEPTANCE OF MATERIAL:-

                 i.   The MPMKVVCL has option to conduct Routine and acceptance test on the samples from the consignment of material received at our consignee Area Stores before its acceptance from the lot supplied against the order. The said test shall be conducted on any one unit of the received lot. The sample Unit shall be selected for conducting Routine and acceptance test from the lot of CTs received in Area stores. The selected samples shall be sent with complete details for testing as per specification at any independent NABL accredited Laboratory like ERDA, CPRI etc.

 

               ii.   In case the sample unit fails in any of the Routine/Acceptance Test the entire lot shall be rejected. The GM/In-charge (stores) shall verify and ensure that the serial number and make is engraved on the sides of the CTs. The testing charges shall be borne by MPMKVVCL. However, in case the material fails in any of the test the entire lot shall be rejected and the entire testing charges shall be recovered from you.

 

             iii.   In case of any dispute relating to test results against the rejected lot and on contesting by the supplier, the re-testing of another randomly selected sample from the rejected lot in presence of your representative may be considered at the discretion of MPMKVVCL, provided request is received from you within days from the date of rejection of material. The testing of sample in presence of your representative may also be considered only when your representative makes its presence on the date of testing of sample at the concern lab. Else the laboratory shall perform testing at its own. The re-testing charges including other expenses viz. transportation, loading unloading etc. shall be borne by you. In case of failure of the lot in second testing, the lot shall be finally rejected and shall have to be lifted and replaced by you. No further representation/ request shall be entertained and the decision/ discretion of MPMKVVCL in this regard shall be final and binding on you.

 

             iv.   In case of emergency, the Purchaser may use/issue material/equipment prior to satisfactory testing at NABL lab. In the event of failure of material/equipment in testing at NABL lab later on following action shall be taken for that particular lot / order only:-

a)      % payment of the bill for the supplies already made will be recovered by Purchaser.

b)      For the material/equipment already used/issued, the guarantee period shall stand twice the normal guarantee period and the period of performance security deposit shall be suitably extended to cover the extended guarantee period.

c)      The purchaser shall be intimated you about the results of such test to make suitable improvement in your manufacturing process and to ensure quality control.

d)      If any quantity is available in the area stores, the same shall not be issued and have to be taken back by you at your own cost.

               v.   The Purchaser has all the rights to conduct the test including type tests, at his own cost by an independent agency during execution of order. In the event of failure in such tests, the expenses incurred in testing shall be your’s account as already mentioned above in case of random testing.
